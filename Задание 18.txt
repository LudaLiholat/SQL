Пример:

Найдите производителей самых дешевых цветных принтеров. Вывести: maker, price

Решение:
SELECT maker, MIN(price) AS MIN_price
FROM Product JOIN Printer 
ON Product.model=Printer.model
WHERE Printer.model IN (SELECT Printer.model
 FROM Product
 WHERE type='Printer'
 )
 AND color='y'AND Printer.price = (SELECT MIN(price) FROM Printer WHERE color='y')
GROUP BY maker
